Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include

Thomas Jefferson
Principal author of the Declaration of Independence and later the 3rd President of the United States.
John Adams
Strong advocate for independence who became the 2nd President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
Respected elder statesman, inventor, and diplomat who helped gain French support during the Revolution.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, famous for his large and bold signature on the Declaration.
Samuel Adams
Revolutionary leader who helped organize the Boston Tea Party and later served as Governor of Massachusetts.

Each of these individuals played a significant role in the founding of the United States and is remembered for their corage in signing the Decleration, and act considered trasonous by the British Crown.
