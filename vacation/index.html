About Us

Budget Vacations started in 2015 with one goal: make travel affordable for everyone. We work directly with local guides, small hotels, and transportation companies to keep costs down while providing memorable and meaningful experiences. Our clients include students, young couples, families, and anyone who loves adventure without luxury-level price tags.
